New premium article: thoughts about service granularity

I’ve written an article about the importance of service granularity in a microservice architecture. This is clearly a controversial topic since a significant fraction of the software development community appears to believe that a single service, i.e. a monolith, is the way to go where as many others appear to be happy with a fine-grained MICROservice architecture that has multiple services per developer.
